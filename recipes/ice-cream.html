Ice Cream in a Bag

Description

This is a quick and fun way to make homemade ice cream without an ice cream maker.
All you need are a few simple ingredients, two plastic bags, and some ice. It’s a great
activity for kids, summer parties, or anytime you want a sweet treat in just a few minutes!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup half-and-half (or whole milk)
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Ice (enough to fill a gallon-sized bag halfway)
  • ⅓ cup salt (rock salt or coarse salt works best)

Instructions:

  1. In a small quart-sized zip-top bag, combine the half-and-half, sugar, and
    vanilla. Seal tightly, removing as much air as possible.
  2. Fill a gallon-sized zip-top bag halfway with ice. Add the salt and mix
    slightly.
  3. Place the sealed smaller bag inside the larger bag with ice and salt.
    Seal the big bag tightly.
  4. Shake and knead the bag for about 10-15 minutes, or until the mixture thickens
    into ice cream. (Use a towel or oven mitts to protect your hands from the cold.)
  5. Remove the small bag, wipe off any salt from the outside, and scoop the ice
    cream into a bowl.

Optional: Add mix-ins like chocolate chips, sprinkles, or fruit after shaking.
